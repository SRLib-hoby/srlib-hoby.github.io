Who We Are

Two Paths, One Purpose

A sculptor who shapes discarded materials into living art, and an event planner who weaves communities together through meaningful action. Together, they prove that creativity and compassion can change the world.

Shaoting · 绍庭

Sculptor & Environmental Artist

An independent sculptor who transforms reclaimed wood, scrap metal, stone, and discarded everyday objects into powerful works of art. His pieces explore the dialogue between humanity and nature — raw, honest, and deeply rooted in the land.

Xiangyi · 相宜

Public Welfare Event Planner

A passionate organizer who designs community-driven charity events, environmental awareness campaigns, and grassroots social initiatives. She believes in the power of gathering people together to spark lasting change.

“We use art not to decorate the world, but to reconnect it — to the earth, to each other, and to what truly matters.”

Our Mission

Art as a Force for Good

We believe that art is not a luxury — it is a language. A language that speaks for the voiceless, heals the overlooked, and inspires communities to reimagine their relationship with the natural world.

Environmental Sustainability

Transforming waste into wonder. Every sculpture begins with materials the world forgot.

Community Empowerment

Art workshops, co-creation projects, and events that bring people together to build something greater.

Art as Social Catalyst

Using creativity to spark dialogue about environmental responsibility and social change.

Cultural Preservation

Honoring traditional craft and local heritage through contemporary artistic expression.

Community & Events

From rice paddies to village schools, our art goes where it's needed most — into the heart of communities, sparking conversations that matter.

Exhibition

Campaign

What Garbage Art?

A provocative community campaign turning everyday waste into thought-provoking art — challenging how we see what we throw away.
